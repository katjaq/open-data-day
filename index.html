Open Data Day

Paris, 7 March 2020

SAVE THE DATE AND JOIN US


International Open Data Day is an annual event that promotes awareness and use of open data. Let's connect with the community around the globe and use open data to build new solutions to issues in our communities. Join us for a hackathon around open data, data analysis and visualisation.

You can select your own dataset, discuss a project and recruit a team of collaborators on site for testing new creative ideas. This workshop is also an opportunity to learn methods, develop skills and collaborate with other participants.
